Enterprise FAQ

Questions a CISO actually asks.

Straight answers to the questions that matter in enterprise procurement.

What does the pilot process look like? +
Our 30-day Enterprise Pilot gives your team full access to all Enterprise capabilities — unlimited connectors, agentic workflows, compliance tools, and a dedicated CSM. We scope the pilot to 2–3 specific use cases (e.g., SRE runbook automation, onboarding acceleration, compliance Q&A) and measure outcomes together. At the end, you have real data to build an internal business case. Pilots are free and convert to annual contracts, not monthly subscriptions.
How long does security review typically take? +
Most enterprise security reviews are completed within 1–2 weeks. We provide a Security & Architecture Overview document that includes data flow diagrams, encryption specifications, deployment architecture, our RBAC model, audit log structure, and compliance readiness posture. We can also schedule a dedicated call with your security team to walk through any questions. For Enterprise+ deployments, we offer additional documentation including penetration test summaries and detailed network architecture diagrams.
Do you hold SOC 2, HIPAA, or FedRAMP certifications? +
We are currently in our SOC 2 Type II audit preparation phase, with the audit scheduled for Q3 2026. Our architecture is SOC 2 compliant by design — AES-256 encryption, immutable audit logging, role-based access control, and least-privilege principles are enforced throughout. HIPAA-compliant deployment architecture is available in Enterprise+. FedRAMP compliance is on our roadmap for Enterprise+ customers in government sectors — contact us for the current timeline and bridge documentation.
Where is my data stored? Can we specify data residency? +
For Enterprise SaaS customers, data is stored in India (Mumbai, AWS) by default. Enterprise+ customers can select their data residency region: India (Mumbai), European Union (Frankfurt), or United States (Virginia). Private cloud and on-premise deployment options (Enterprise+) allow data to remain entirely within your own infrastructure, with zero data leaving your perimeter.
How does pricing scale with organization size? +
Pricing is structured around seat count, deployment model, and the specific capabilities your organization requires. We do not publish a self-serve pricing grid — pricing is always determined in conversation with your team. What we can say: we structure deals with volume tiers, and annual contracts include meaningful discounts vs. theoretical monthly equivalents. The minimum engagement is typically 100 seats for Enterprise, with no upper limit.
Can we run PlugAI on-premise with our own LLMs? +
Yes — this is one of PlugAI's most significant differentiators. Enterprise+ supports full on-premise or private cloud deployment, including support for local LLMs (Llama 3, Mistral, Codestral) for completely air-gapped environments. Your AI queries never leave your data center. This is especially relevant for defense, financial services, and healthcare organizations with strict data sovereignty requirements. Contact us for deployment architecture specifics and infrastructure requirements.
What does typical onboarding look like? +
Onboarding is a four-week structured program led by your dedicated Customer Success Manager. Week 1: connector setup and initial knowledge ingestion. Week 2: RBAC configuration and user provisioning. Week 3: agent workflow configuration for your primary use cases. Week 4: admin training, reporting setup, and go-live. For Enterprise+ private cloud deployments, infrastructure provisioning typically adds 2–4 weeks, depending on your environment. We provide runbooks and hands-on support throughout.
