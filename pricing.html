Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions, straight answers.

Everything you need to know about PlugAI pricing and plans.

How does seat-based pricing work? +
You pay a flat monthly fee per user (seat) in your organization. There are no per-query, per-API-call, or per-document fees. Whether a user makes 10 searches or 10,000, the cost stays the same. You only pay for active seats, and you can add or remove seats at any time from your billing dashboard.
Can I switch plans at any time? +
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to the new features and we prorate the charge for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing period, so you keep your current features until then.
What's included in the free Starter tier? +
The Starter plan is free forever for up to 20 users. It includes 1,000 articles, 5 connectors (Slack, Confluence, Google Drive, etc.), 1 workspace, basic semantic search, AI chat with multi-LLM support, and community support. It's a fully functional knowledge base -- not a trial -- so there's no time limit.
Do you offer annual billing discounts? +
Yes. Annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly pricing. Professional drops to $240/user/year (equivalent to $20/user/month), and Enterprise drops to $528/user/year (equivalent to $44/user/month). Annual plans are billed upfront for the full year.
What is Enterprise+ licensing? +
Enterprise+ is our top-tier offering for organizations with advanced requirements: multi-org licensing, private cloud or on-premise deployment, local/private LLMs for air-gapped environments, FedRAMP compliance, custom SLAs, encryption key management, and a dedicated support team. Pricing is custom based on your deployment scope. Contact our sales team for a tailored quote.
Is there a setup fee or implementation cost? +
No setup fees for Starter, Professional, or Enterprise plans. You can self-serve onboarding in minutes. For Enterprise+ deployments that require private cloud configuration, custom integrations, or dedicated training, there may be a one-time implementation fee -- your account team will outline this during the scoping process.
What LLM providers are supported? +
All plans include access to 6 LLM providers: Anthropic (Claude Opus, Sonnet, Haiku), OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4o Mini, o3 Mini), Google Gemini (2.5 Pro, 2.5 Flash), AWS Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, and local models (Llama 3, Mistral, Codestral). Enterprise+ adds support for fully private/air-gapped LLM deployments.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education? +
Yes. We offer special pricing for qualified nonprofits, educational institutions, and open-source projects. Contact our sales team with your organization details and we'll work out a plan that fits your budget.
